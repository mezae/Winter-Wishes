Mass E-mailing: Creating Templates

This page will help you create templates that will be used to e-mail all or some the agencies participating in this year's Winter Wishes.

To add a new template, simply click . Next, in the sidebar, type in a name for your new template and a short summary of the intended message. When you click save a new box will appear onscreen to represent your new template. Click on the template name to see the template and add your message.
