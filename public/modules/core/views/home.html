Welcome to Winter Wishes 2015!

Login to start working on your tracking form.

Login
Write

First, get all participants at your organization to write their letters. Make sure they add fun facts about themselves and festive decorations. We want the volunteers to be excited about receiving a letter.

Organize

Once you have collected all letters, organize them by tracking number. This will make the rest of the process easier.

Review & Enter

Next, review each letter to make sure the gift request is reasonable. Please keep in mind the $40 limit. If the letter looks good, type it in the appropriate tracking form slot. Otherwise, start a "To Be Rewritten" pile. Sub-par letters will be returned and the participants may end up without a gift.

Submit

Finally, when all letters have been reviewed and entered, submit the tracking form online and mail the letters to the New York Cares office.
