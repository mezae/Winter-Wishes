Ready, Go!

This page will help you easily create and manage tracking forms for each agency accepted into the Winter Wishes program this year. Let's get started!

First, let's set the due date for the tracking forms:

In order to proceed, all Winter Wishes applications in salesforce should be processed, and you should have the following information for each accepted agency:

  • Agency Code
  • Agency Name
  • Contact Name
  • Contact E-mail
  • Accepted Children
  • Accepted Teens
  • Accepted Seniors

Download a salesforce report with these fields, and save it as a csv file.

Click to begin. Then, click and find the csv file saved on your computer. Finally, click and the accepted agencies should appear onscreen. Each agency will be represented like this:

100 Letters

The and only appear when you hover over an agency.

Click in the box of the tracking form you wish to edit. A form will appear populated with the current information for the agency you selected. Once you have made the necessary modifications, click to safeguard your changes.

* If you forgot to add an agency, just click for a blank form.

Click in the box of the tracking form you wish to delete. This will delete everything associated with the agency. This cannot be undone. For this reason, you will be asked to confirm that you really want to delete the selected agency.

Click in the box of the tracking form you wish to see. You can access an individual tracking form anytime to check how much progress a particular agency contact has made. However, this feature is more useful once an agency has submitted their form and you are ready to review it. The color of the button changes based on what stage of the process the tracking form is in.

means the community partner has not yet started working on it.

means the community partner has begun entering data.

means the community partner has submitted it and it is ready for review.

means you have begun reviewing the tracking form.

means you have finished reviewing the tracking form.

Once all the tracking forms have been created, you will need to e-mail all accepted agencies to let them know that they have been accepted into this year's Winter Wishes program and give them further instructions. For help with that, head over to the E-mail Templates in the main menu.

You can also access this Tutorial through the main menu if you need to see it again.
